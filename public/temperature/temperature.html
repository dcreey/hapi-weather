Below is an interactive visualization of the min and max temperatures for the first 11 months of 2015 on a given day. You may:
  • Highlight a section of the lower graph to zoom in on a range of dates in the upper graph. Drag that selection to change the range of dates.
  • Click on the legend key icon to hide or display the associated line.
  • Use the dropdown below to change the frequency by which the temperatures are averaged over (day, week, month).
  • Hover over the larger graph to view temperature values.
